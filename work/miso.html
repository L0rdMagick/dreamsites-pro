The Challenge

Miso Noodle Bar makes excellent fresh, hand-pulled ramen. Despite having a loyal dining customer base, their online ordering was processed through third-party food delivery apps that took huge 20% to 30% commission cuts and failed to display their food beautifully. They wanted a branded ordering flow that bypassed middleman fees.

The Strategy

We built a direct, commission-free online ordering app natively integrated into their web experience. The interface features their high-quality food photography and lets users configure orders and customize toppings (e.g., adding extra soft-boiled eggs or chashu pork) with a simple tap, checking out securely via Apple Pay or credit card.

Business Operations & Integrations

Operating a restaurant requires speed. Orders must arrive in the kitchen instantly without manual typing or delays:

  • Stripe Terminal POS Printer Sync: When a user checks out using Apple Pay or credit card, the site sends a webhook directly to the kitchen POS. The order is printed directly on the kitchen prep line ticket printer.
  • Mapbox Distance Calculation API: During delivery orders, the app calculates the distance from the restaurant to the client's home in real-time, validating delivery radius limits and calculating courier fees.

The Outcome

Direct online ordering sales increased by 45% within three months of launch. Because they bypassed third-party app fees, Miso Noodle Bar saved thousands of dollars in commissions, completely recouping the cost of their custom website build in under six weeks.
