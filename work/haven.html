The Challenge

Haven Hair Studio is a boutique salon that was struggling with inefficient appointment booking and late cancellations. They were using a third-party booking portal that felt disjointed from their brand, looked clunky on mobile, and charged high monthly fees while failing to secure credit card deposits to prevent no-shows.

The Strategy

We designed a bespoke booking experience native to their website. Instead of sending users to an external software platform, the site uses a custom calendar integration. This makes scheduling a stylist and picking a slot as frictionless as possible. The booking widget collects card details directly to guarantee the slot, maintaining a premium feeling throughout.

The Design

We used a soft, warm cream and pastel pink aesthetic that feels welcoming and clean. Typography details use elegant serifs. The custom booking interface on mobile fits perfectly in one screen thumb-reach, allowing guests to choose services, stylists, and slots in less than 60 seconds.

The Results

No-shows have dropped to absolute zero since implementing guaranteed deposits. Guests have repeatedly commented on how much they love booking online now, resulting in a 40% increase in bookings made outside salon working hours.
