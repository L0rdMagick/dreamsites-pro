Self-Diagnostic

Diagnose your digital bottlenecks.

Most website performance flaws are invisible to the business owner but highly obvious to prospective clients and search engines. Spend 2 minutes running this self-diagnostic audit to grade your current site's layout, speed, and automations.

1. Speed Performance

Does your site load fully in under 2.0 seconds on mobile viewports?

2. Search Visibility

Does your business rank on page 1 of Google for your core keywords?

3. Scheduling & Bookings

Can clients book consultations or appointments directly on your site?

4. Mobile Design Quality

Is the layout responsive on all phones without cut-off elements?

5. Custom Brand Identity

Is your website completely free of generic, cookie-cutter templates?

6. Interactive Tools

Does your site feature interactive tools (like calculators or custom estimators) to engage and convert visitors?

7. Modern Visual Design

Does your design use modern aesthetics (smooth animations, custom typography, micro-interactions) rather than feeling dated?

8. Client-Centric Sales Funnel

Does your website copy focus on solving client pain points with clear CTAs guiding them to action?
